# TradePilot AI™ Master Roadmap

## Product Vision

TradePilot AI is the intelligent operating system for modern trade businesses. It captures every enquiry, qualifies each lead, scores commercial value, supports quote conversion, routes work to the right people, and produces clear operational intelligence.

The platform must support three stages of growth:

1. **Local Operator** — power one premium local multi-trade business.
2. **Franchise Network** — replicate the operating model across territories.
3. **SaaS Platform** — license the lead, CRM, automation, and intelligence system to other service businesses.
